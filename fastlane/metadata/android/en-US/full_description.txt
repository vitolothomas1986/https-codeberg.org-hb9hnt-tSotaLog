tSotaLog is a simple FLOSS Ham Radio app specifically desgined to log SOTA (Summit on the Air)
QSOs. It is a fork from the more general TOTALOG which is designed to log for small contests
and portable operation. (Also available on F-Droid)

Features include

* Logging SOTA QSOs
* Logging Summit-to-Summit contacts
* Editing QSOs
* Storing incomplete QSOs and editing them later (useful in a pile-up)
* Exporting a CSV to import into SotaData
* Automatically rewrite summit-reference hbsz020 as HB/SZ-020
